CHEESE

Organic Cheese

Mossfield Organic Farm is using the freshest milk with minimal processing, their bottled milk and natural yogurt retain their natural goodness without homogenization or added milk powders.

Cheeses Offered:

Mossfield Sleive Bloom: A mild, creamy cheddar-type cheese, their newest addition.

Mossfield Mature: A crumbly cheese aged for at least eight months.

Mossfield Young: A creamy cheese available from sixteen weeks, with herbed varieties like Tomato & Herbs, Garlic & Basil, and Cumin Seed.

All cheeses are handmade in small batches, suitable for vegetarians, and offer a delightful range of flavors to excite any taste buds.

BREAD

Organic Bread

We proudly offer a selection of freshly baked organic bread, crafted with care and dedication. Our self-baked organic bread is made using traditional methods and the finest organic ingredients, ensuring a wholesome and delicious taste in every loaf.

In addition to our own baked goods, we partner with Coolfin Organic Bakery to bring you an even wider variety of organic bread. Coolfin Organic Bakery is renowned for its commitment to quality and sustainability, producing bread that is not only delicious but also ethically made.

Whether you prefer a classic loaf, a hearty whole grain, or something more unique, our organic bread selection has something to satisfy every palate. Enjoy the taste of freshly baked goodness and the peace of mind that comes with choosing organic.
