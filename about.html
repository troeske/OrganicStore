About Us

Welcome to Our Organic Store in Ireland!

At our organic store, we are passionate about bringing you the finest organic groceries straight from the heart of Ireland. Our commitment to quality and sustainability is evident in every product we offer, sourced directly from our family-owned Mossfield Organic Farm and other trusted local organic farms.


Our Story

Nestled in the lush countryside, Mossfield Organic Farm has been in our family for generations. We take immense pride in our sustainable farming practices, ensuring that every item we grow is nurtured with care and respect for the environment. Our dedication to organic farming means no synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs) are used, ensuring that what you bring home is as pure and natural as nature intended.

Supporting Local Farms

We believe in supporting our community, which is why we partner with other local organic farms to bring you a diverse array of products. These partnerships ensure that you not only get the best groceries but also help sustain small-scale farmers who share our commitment to organic farming and environmental stewardship.


Visit Us Today

Come and explore our organic store, where every purchase supports sustainable farming practices and promotes a healthier lifestyle. Our friendly staff is here to help you find exactly what you need and share our love for organic farming.


Thank you for choosing to shop local and organic. Together, we can make a difference, one delicious bite at a time.
